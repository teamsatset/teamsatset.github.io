Privacy Policy — Buygen TextPad

Provided by BuygenDev. This Privacy Policy explains how Buygen TextPad handles user data and privacy.

Effective / Last Updated: November 25, 2025
Developer: BuygenDev

1. Scope and Acceptance

This Privacy Policy applies to Buygen TextPad (the “Application”), a lightweight text editor developed and distributed by BuygenDev. By downloading, installing, or using the Application, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information Collection

Buygen TextPad does not collect, upload, transmit, or store any personal data, usage metrics, or written content from the user’s device. All user-generated content (including text files, notes, and other documents) is stored exclusively on the user’s device.

3. Local Storage and Data Management

The Application stores files and settings locally on the device file system. Users are solely responsible for backing up, exporting, or deleting their files. The developer (BuygenDev) has no access to user files or contents stored on a user’s device unless the user explicitly sends such files to the developer.

4. Internet & Network Access

Buygen TextPad is designed to operate entirely offline. The Application does not require an internet connection for its core features and does not communicate with external servers or services as part of normal operation.

5. Third-Party Services, Advertising, and Analytics

The Application does not integrate third-party advertising networks, analytics services, or tracking SDKs. No personal data, telemetry, or usage information is sent to any third party.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Because no user data is collected or transmitted by the Application, BuygenDev does not share user data with third parties. The developer will not disclose user content unless required by law and where legally compelled to do so.

7. Children's Privacy

The Application does not intentionally collect information from children. Since no data is collected, the Application does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or local equivalent).

8. Security

All data remains under the user’s control on their device. Users should follow best practices to secure their device and local files (for example, using device-level passcodes, file encryption, and regular backups).

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

BuygenDev may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the effective date above will be updated. Continued use of the Application after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
